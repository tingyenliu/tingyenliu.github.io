Introduction

Why did you go to Busan?

As I said before, I don't have much opportunities to travel. I was looking for a place where's culture, language are different from us, but I can get there easy.

Korea is not far from Taiwan. The public security is good, and the transportation is convenient. The most important thing is Korean are so kind! I have a good memory when I went to Korea for the first time.

What language did you speak when you were in Busan?

At first, I speak English and Japanese for communication. To my surprise, a lot of Korean can speak Chinese, even they are just clerks in convenience store.

Sometimes, I encountered some situations that we cannot communicate by languages. I don't know their languages, and they don't speak the languages I speak. Therefore, body language is the best way to communicate.
